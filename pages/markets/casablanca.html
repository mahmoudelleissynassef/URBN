The Casablanca office market

Casablanca is Morocco's economic and financial capital and the country's primary office market. Casablanca Finance City (CFC) is the flagship financial hub and a recognised gateway for multinationals expanding into Francophone and West Africa, anchoring strong demand from banking, insurance, offshoring and logistics occupiers. That demand has spread modern Grade A development across CFC, the corporate and offshoring cluster at Sidi Maarouf, and the established business addresses of Anfa, Maarif and Gauthier. For occupiers, that means a genuine choice between modern build-to-suit towers, fitted floors, serviced suites and flexible coworking.

Because rents are usually quoted in Moroccan dirham (MAD) and increasingly in EUR or USD for prime stock, and because fit-out condition varies widely, comparing options on a like-for-like basis is hard. Prime office districts in Casablanca

Where to base your team depends on commute, client proximity and budget. The main clusters:

Casablanca Finance City (CFC)

The flagship financial hub and a recognised gateway into Francophone and West Africa — modern Grade A towers and the highest concentration of multinational HQs and regional offices.

Sidi Maarouf

The corporate and offshoring cluster, home to large back-office, BPO and technology operations in purpose-built business parks south of the centre.

Anfa

Prestige residential-and-business district, favoured by finance, professional services and corporate headquarters seeking established, well-located addresses.

Maarif

Central, vibrant commercial district with a mix of office buildings and retail, popular with SMEs, agencies and growing teams.

Gauthier

Well-connected city-centre district with a mix of converted and purpose-built offices, suited to professional services and creative firms.

Ain Sebaa

Established industrial and logistics-led district to the east, practical for operations, distribution and back-office teams that need value and access.

Casablanca Finance City (CFC), Sidi Maarouf, Anfa, Maarif, Gauthier and Ain Sebaa. The best fit depends on your team's commute, clients and budget.
