The Cairo office market

Cairo is the largest office market in North Africa and the corporate gateway to Egypt's 100-million-plus economy. Demand from multinationals, regional headquarters, technology firms and the BPO/offshoring sector has pushed Grade A development east toward New Cairo and west toward Sheikh Zayed and 6th of October, while the historic Nile-front CBD retains prestige addresses. For occupiers, that means a genuine choice between modern build-to-suit towers, fitted floors, serviced suites and flexible coworking.

Because rents are usually quoted in Egyptian pounds (EGP) and increasingly in USD for prime stock, and because fit-out condition varies widely, comparing options on a like-for-like basis is hard. URBN does that work for you — confirming what is actually available, at what price, and on what terms.

Prime office districts in Cairo

Where to base your team depends on commute, client proximity and budget. The main clusters:

New Cairo & Fifth Settlement

The dominant Grade A submarket — modern towers, business parks and the highest concentration of multinational HQs and serviced offices.

Nile CBD & Downtown

Prestige Nile-front addresses and established corporate towers, favoured by finance, legal and government-facing firms.

Sheikh Zayed & 6th of October

Fast-growing west-side hub with newer Grade A space, good value and access for teams living in west Cairo and Giza.

Maadi

Leafy, established district popular with NGOs, tech and energy firms, with a mix of converted villas and purpose-built offices.

Heliopolis & Nasr City

Central, well-connected and close to the airport — practical mid-market office space for regional and back-office teams.

New Administrative Capital

Emerging government and corporate district east of New Cairo, with new-build Grade A stock coming to market.

Types of office space in Cairo

Grade A & whole-floor

Full floors and HQ space in modern towers for established teams that want their own identity and long-term certainty.

Serviced & managed offices

Private, fully fitted suites with reception, meeting rooms and IT included — move-in ready on flexible terms.

Coworking & flexible desks

Hot desks and dedicated desks for startups, project teams and satellite offices that need to scale up or down.

Cairo office space — FAQs

How much does office space cost in Cairo?

Indicative Grade A asking rents are roughly EGP 350–700 / m² / month in prime districts like New Cairo and the Nile CBD; serviced offices and coworking are priced per desk. Real pricing depends on grade, fit-out, floor and term — we confirm live figures per option.

Which are the best areas for offices in Cairo?

New Cairo & the Fifth Settlement, the Nile CBD & Downtown, Sheikh Zayed & 6th of October, Maadi, and Heliopolis/Nasr City. The best fit depends on your team's commute, clients and budget.

Is coworking space available in Cairo?

Yes — a growing coworking and serviced-office market, concentrated in New Cairo, Sheikh Zayed and Maadi, with hot desks, dedicated desks and private managed suites on flexible terms.
