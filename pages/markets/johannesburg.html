The Johannesburg office market

Johannesburg is Africa's financial capital and the continent's largest corporate office market. It is home to the Johannesburg Stock Exchange — based in Sandton — and the deepest pool of multinational headquarters anywhere in Africa. Demand from financial services, mining houses, professional services and technology firms has concentrated Grade A development in Sandton and the surrounding northern nodes of Rosebank, Bryanston and Illovo, with Midrand serving the corridor toward Pretoria. For occupiers, that means a genuine choice between modern Grade A towers, fitted floors, serviced suites and flexible coworking.

Because rents are usually quoted in South African rand (ZAR) on a gross basis, and because fit-out condition, parking ratios and operating costs vary widely between nodes, comparing options on a like-for-like basis is hard. Prime office nodes in Johannesburg

Where to base your team depends on commute, client proximity and budget. The main clusters:

Sandton

The prime CBD and financial core — home to the JSE, the highest concentration of multinational HQs, Grade A towers and serviced offices in the country.

Rosebank

A revitalised, well-connected node with newer Grade A stock, Gautrain access and a strong mix of corporate, media and creative occupiers.

Bryanston

A leafy northern node favoured for campus-style offices and good value relative to Sandton, popular with technology and services firms.

Illovo

A compact, established node between Sandton and Rosebank, with boutique Grade A buildings popular with finance, legal and advisory firms.

Midrand

Strategically placed on the corridor between Johannesburg and Pretoria, offering newer, value-oriented Grade A space and easy highway access.

Central CBD & Braamfontein

The historic city centre and adjacent Braamfontein — practical, well-connected space for government-facing and back-office teams.

Johannesburg office space — FAQs

How much does office space cost in Johannesburg?

Indicative prime Grade A asking rents are roughly ZAR 150–250 / m² / month (gross) in prime nodes like Sandton and Rosebank; serviced offices and coworking are priced per desk. Real pricing depends on grade, fit-out, floor and term — we confirm live figures per option.

Which are the best areas for offices in Johannesburg?

Sandton (the prime financial core and home of the JSE), Rosebank, Bryanston, Illovo and Midrand. The best fit depends on your team's commute, clients and budget.

Is coworking space available in Johannesburg?

Yes — a deep coworking and serviced-office market, concentrated in Sandton, Rosebank and Bryanston, with hot desks, dedicated desks and private managed suites on flexible terms.
