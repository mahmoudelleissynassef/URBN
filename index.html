How URBN Works

01

Browse Verified Offices

02

Shortlist Buildings

Filter by city, district, floor plate, parking, and fit-out condition. Save buildings to a shortlist and compare options. Or request a managed Market Scan for a curated shortlist within 48 hours.

Why Corporate Occupiers Use URBN

01

Fragmented broker markets

In Cairo, Lagos, or Nairobi, quality availability is dispersed across dozens of local brokers with no centralised source. URBN consolidates and verifies.

02

Inconsistent listing data

Floor plates, parking ratios, and availability are rarely current in secondary markets. URBN applies a direct verification standard before any building is listed.

03

Opaque access to supply

The best buildings in Africa deal through relationships, not marketing. URBN provides structured access to supply that would otherwise take months to discover.

04

Introductions need protecting

Without a formal introduction record, commissions are disputed. URBN's protected introduction system is built into the workflow, not an afterthought.
