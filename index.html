The Process

How URBN Works

01

Browse Verified Offices

Access a curated inventory of Grade A and A+ office buildings across 13 markets. Every listing is verified directly against the source — no recycled broker data, no stale availability figures.

02

Shortlist & Compare

Filter by area, district, floor plate, parking, and operational requirements. Save buildings to your shortlist and compare options side by side. Request a managed Market Scan if you prefer a curated approach.

03

Request a Protected Introduction

When you are ready, URBN formally introduces you to the building. The introduction is timestamped, logged, and commission-protected for 24 months. You do not contact the landlord directly — the platform manages the process.

Why URBN

Built for Corporate Occupiers

01

Fragmented broker markets

In Cairo, Lagos, or Nairobi, quality availability is scattered across dozens of local brokers with no centralised source. URBN consolidates and verifies.

02

Inconsistent listing quality

Floor plates, parking ratios, and availability are rarely current in secondary markets. URBN applies a direct verification standard before any building is listed.

03

Opaque access to supply

The best buildings in Africa often deal through relationships, not marketing. URBN provides structured access to supply that would otherwise take months to discover.

04

Introductions need protecting

Without a formal introduction record, commissions get disputed. URBN's protected introduction system is embedded in the workflow, not added as an afterthought.
